About Me

I'm a biomedical signal processing and machine learning engineer, currently pursuing my M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering – Integrated Circuits of Electronic Systems at K. N. Toosi University of Technology. My research focuses on robust fetal heart rate monitoring using fECG via enhanced ICA and time series signal modeling.

I specialize in developing algorithms for wearable health-monitoring devices, achieving 99% F1-score in fetal ECG detection. My expertise spans from low-level VHDL design to high-level machine learning implementations using PyTorch, TensorFlow, and MATLAB.
